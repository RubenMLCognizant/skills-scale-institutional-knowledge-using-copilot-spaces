# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
Translates user and business requirements into usable, effective interfaces. Advocates for the end-user and collaborates throughout delivery to ensure design intent is met.

### Responsibilities
- Design wireframes, user flows, mockups, and prototypes aligned with acceptance criteria
- Refine designs based on team feedback, user research, and usability testing
- Collaborate with developers, product managers, and QA to ensure implementation matches design
- Participate in design reviews, sprint planning, and solution validation
- Document design decisions and create design systems/patterns for consistency

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences that drive adoption
- Reduce friction and improve customer satisfaction
- Translate business goals into user-centric features

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with engineers and product managers
- Participates in sprint planning and product demos
- Shares design rationale and user research insights
- Contributes to documentation and design guidelines

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Developers:** Collaborate on implementation feasibility and technical constraints
- **Product Managers:** Align on user research findings and design priorities
- **QA/Testing:** Ensure designs are testable and acceptance criteria are clear

---

## DevOps / Platform Engineer

### Role Summary
Ensures environment consistency, deployment reliability, and automates operational processes for delivery and quality. Enables safe, fast deployments and operational visibility.

### Responsibilities
- Manage CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code (IaC), and environment configurations
- Support developers in troubleshooting build, deploy, and integration issues
- Automate testing, monitoring, security scanning, and observability
- Drive incident response processes and support post-incident retrospectives
- Advise on technical feasibility, scalability, and security trade-offs

### Goals
- Enable rapid, low-risk deployments with confidence
- Ensure platform stability, scalability, and security
- Minimize manual operational overhead and toil
- Improve observability and incident response times

### Typical Communication
- Coordinates release processes with PM, developers, and QA
- Shares monitoring dashboards, alerts, and incident postmortems
- Participates in planning to raise infrastructure and deployment risks
- Documents deployment runbooks and rollback procedures

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Developers:** Troubleshoots CI/CD issues and advises on deployment patterns
- **Project Managers:** Escalates infrastructure blockers and release risks
- **QA/Testing:** Collaborates on automated testing infrastructure and environment provisioning

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Bridges the gap between business needs and technical implementation. Ensures requirements are clear, prioritized, validated, and understood by all stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit detailed functional and non-functional requirements from business stakeholders
- Collaborate on backlog refinement, prioritization, and acceptance criteria definition
- Validate delivered solutions against business goals and user acceptance criteria
- Create and maintain requirements documentation and decision logs
- Facilitate stakeholder alignment workshops and feedback sessions

### Goals
- Reduce ambiguity on what needs to be built and why
- Accelerate stakeholder alignment and avoid costly rework
- Support prioritization based on business value and risk
- Ensure delivered work meets both business and user needs

### Typical Communication
- Leads requirements workshops with business and technical teams
- Participates in planning, sprint reviews, and retrospectives
- Maintains living documentation and decision logs
- Provides traceability between business requirements and delivered features

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Product Managers:** Collaborates on requirements refinement and prioritization
- **Developers:** Clarifies acceptance criteria and validates technical feasibility
- **Project Managers:** Supports scope management and change management

---

## Change Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinates and communicates the impact of team, process, or product changes across the organization. Ensures smooth transitions and adoption.

### Responsibilities
- Develop comprehensive change plans and communication timelines
- Identify and engage stakeholders who will be impacted by changes
- Facilitate training, documentation, and support adoption of new processes or tools
- Collect feedback and concerns from impacted teams
- Track change implementation and resolve adoption barriers

### Goals
- Minimize disruption and resistance during organizational or product changes
- Increase stakeholder buy-in and clarity around the rationale for changes
- Ensure compliance, proper documentation, and knowledge transfer
- Support rapid, confident adoption of changes

### Typical Communication
- Change announcements and rollout timelines (email, town halls, meetings)
- Feedback channels and listening sessions for impacted teams
- Training materials and FAQs
- Post-implementation surveys and retrospectives

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Project Managers:** Coordinates change rollout with project schedules and milestones
- **Product Managers:** Communicates product changes and rationale to users/stakeholders
- **All Roles:** Supports adoption of process changes and new ways of working

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Acts as the primary liaison between delivery teams and the support function. Brings customer and support insights into planning and ensures operational readiness for releases.

### Responsibilities
- Provide support team input during project planning and backlog refinement
- Coordinate rollout communications, runbooks, and readiness planning for releases
- Track common support issues and escalations to inform continuous improvement
- Participate in retrospectives to identify support-related process improvements
- Support knowledge transfer and documentation for new features

### Goals
- Improve post-release user outcomes and support team readiness
- Reduce recurring incidents and support burden over time
- Ensure smooth, well-documented deployments with minimal support impact
- Drive continuous improvement based on real user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Weekly handoffs with developers and product managers pre/post release
- Contributes to runbooks, FAQs, and customer documentation
- Attends sprint reviews and retrospectives
- Escalates critical user feedback to product and engineering leadership

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Project Managers:** Provides schedule input and ensures support is included in release planning
- **Developers:** Collaborates on technical documentation and troubleshooting guidance
- **QA/Testing:** Aligns on acceptance criteria and supports readiness testing
